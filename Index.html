Thinking about having fun in the water?

Check the water discharge of the Rhein in Rotterdam for the perfect boating adventure!

Current Week:

"River discharge max" and "min" refer to the maximum and minimum amount of water flowing through a river over a specific period, measured in cubic meters per second (m³/s)."

Low water level: shallow water is the motto.

Up to a river speed of 30 m³/s, it is safe for boaters of all experience levels. When the Rhine is flowing at this speed, it's perfect for a relaxing day on the water! Here are some ideas for you and your friends.

  • Rubber boat: What could be better than drifting down the river with friends and good music? The water has the perfect speed to let yourself drift with the tide.
  • Go kayaking or canoeing: Take advantage of the tide for an exciting paddle on the river.
  • Pack your picnic, your friends and your jukebox and off you go on your river adventure. Let yourself drift and enjoy the sun on the water.

Water level high: Let’s take the big boats!

At a river speed of over 30 m³/s, it is too unsafe for the rubber boat on the water because the water of the Rhine in Rotterdam is flowing very fast: Unfortunately, you'll have to leave your small boat at home, but what about something even bigger? Here are some ideas on how you can still have fun on the water!

  • Rowing boat: A rowing boat offers more stability and safety than a rubber boat. So don't hesitate to row out onto the river!
  • Motorboat: Even without a boating license, you can hire a smaller motorboat and spend an unforgettable day with friends and family on the water.

What are you waiting for? Off to the Rhein!
